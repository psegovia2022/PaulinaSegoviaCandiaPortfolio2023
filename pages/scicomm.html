Cientificamente Mujeres

Científicamente Mujeres is a scientific communication initiative. It was created and founded by Rocio Mayol Troncoso who invited me to participate as co-founder.

Our goal is to increase the participation of women in STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) and communicate research carried out by female scientists in a friendly and understandable space for all.

The following are examples of some of the conversations I have had. I invite you to visit our social media to learn further about what we do. We have talked with more than 60 scientists coming from many different scientific domains.

It is important to mention that all of them are in Spanish.
