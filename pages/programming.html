Nervetools Proyect

Nerve Tools is an open-source project that aims to democratize access to science.

Dr. Marcelo Aguilar-Rivera commissioned me to develop this website to share the devices designed and developed by him after being published in scientific journals.

His goal is to make this valuable material available to scientists and enthusiast students, especially in undeveloped countries, to build and design bioinstruments if needed.
